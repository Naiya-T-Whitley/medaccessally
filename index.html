I'm Naiya Whitley, a non-clinical Access & Adherence Systems Manager here at MedAccess Ally. //based in Eastern North Carolina//. I help individuals simplify medication access, stay consistent with prescriptions, and navigate the healthcare system without feeling overwhelmed.

I specialize in helping individuals and caregivers feel more organized, supported, and confident with their medication routines and access needs. I believe everyone deserves clarity - not confusion - when it comes to their. My approach is simple:

  • Actively listen to your need

You don't need to have everything figured out. I'm here to make the process lighter, simpler, and stress-free.

MedAccess Ally

MedAccess Ally is a support-based service designed to bridge the gap between you, your medications, and your day-to-day routines.

Our mission is to make access and adherence easier through clear guidance, practical tools, and judgement free support. we focus on:

  • Medication access (refills, changes, delivery, transitions)
  • Adherence support
  • Organized routines
  • Simple tools to help you stay on track
  • Reducing stess, confusion, and delays
MedAccess Ally exists to make your journey smoother - one clear step at a time.

We provide structured guidance and support, We do not give medical advice, prescribe medication, or change doses. Our role is to help you stay organized, consistent, and empowered with your medications.

    • Coordinate with pharmacies and providers
    • Guide you through insurance and access barriers

How I Support YOU!

As your Case Manager, I am your direct point of contact throughout your experience. I help you:

  • Understand what needs attention
  • Simplify Communication
  • Organize Medication Information
  • Create Personalized Reminders, Calendars, or Checklists
  • Navigate Access Issues
  • Feel Supported, Heard, and Guided Every Step Of The Way
My goal is to help you gain stability, confidence, and clarity without feelings rushed or judged.
